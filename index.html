About

Khat Al Salama School Transport

Khat Al Salama School Transport is a leading company in school bus rental in accordance with the highest international standards approved by the Department of Transport in the United Arab Emirates. Whereas the company is distinguished by providing the latest buses manufactured in the field of transportation, equipped with the latest special technologies for providing a safe and comfortable environment for students inside the school bus.

Receiving school students and ensuring their safety and safe arrival to the arrival point.

Regular meetings with the drivers.

Ensure that the school buses are empty of students after their disembarkation.

Our Vision

Providing a distinguished, safe and comfortable service in the school transportation sector by providing the latest buses and technical equipment and continuous follow-up to the latest regulations issued by the Official Departments and conducting permanent qualifying and training for the employees of the company for honing their skills and increasing their acquired experiences.

Our Company

Our Company is characterized as

A National Company accredited by the State and having a selection of qualified Arab and foreign competencies.

The company adheres to the legal and professional standards related to the school transport operators.

Selecting drivers in accordance with the highest required standards and those with a high level of skills and physical and health fitness.

We are concerned with promoting fruitful and serious cooperation with the School Administration for conducting the work as required.

We are characterized by permanent and continuous follow-up with the Department of Transport to know all that is new in school transport and how to develop and improve it.

We provides qualified supervisors on buses (as per agreement) who have the necessary training courses for the school transport service and the service inside schools.

The latest fleet of school buses has state-of-the-art models.

We also conduct periodic medical examinations to ensure their safety.

OUR SERVICES

Our Services include

1

Comprehensive insurance for buses, students, drivers and supervisors.

2

Providing an alternative bus with the driver and supervisor as quickly as possible (in case of any emergency on the road).

3

Providing all buses with all safety and security requirements in accordance with the terms and requirements of the Department of Transport.

4

The Company has advanced electronic records to monitor school buses round trip according to the specified time in coordination with the School Administration.

5

Dealing with a safety system issued by an integrated transportation center (Department of transport).

6

Having the latest surveillance cameras and remote tracking devices in school buses.
